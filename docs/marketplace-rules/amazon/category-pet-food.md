## TL;DR

Pet food — отдельная regulated category. Frozen pet food (Freshpet) — особо strict. Требует ungating + специальный compliance docs. Vladimir's гипотетические pet bundles — second priority после grocery.

---

## Hard rules

### 1. Storage temperature

- Frozen pet food (Freshpet) → `Frozen` + cooler shipping
- Refrigerated treats (some brands) → `Refrigerated`
- Dry kibble / treats → `Ambient`

### 2. AAFCO compliance

Pet food sold in US должен соответствовать AAFCO (Association of American Feed Control Officials) labeling. Это manufacturer's responsibility, но Bundle Factory должна сохранять original packaging внутри bundle box.

### 3. Approval (Ungating)

Pet Food = restricted category. Каждый account нужно отдельно apply. Чем "более регулированный" sub-category — тем больше docs нужно.

### 4. Image / labeling

Bundles должны clearly show pet food content. Misleading marketing (e.g. human food в pet bundle) — violation.

### 5. Allergen / ingredient disclosure

Для pet food обязательно — ingredient list, guaranteed analysis, feeding instructions. Сохраняется original packaging внутри bundle.
