# Amazon Brand Registry Benefits

> **Source:** https://brandservices.amazon.com/
> **Last verified:** 2026-05-17
> **Priority:** P1 (Vladimir уже имеет Brand Registry — это документ для context)

---

## TL;DR

Brand Registry = программа защиты brand-owners на Amazon. Vladimir's Salutem Vita registered на Salutem Solutions, Starfit registered на Sirius International. Benefits включают: protection от unauthorized sellers, доступ к A+ Content, Sponsored Brand ads, Brand Story module, и GTIN exemption application.

---

## Key benefits

### 1. Brand protection

- **Blocks unauthorized resellers** на ваших ASINs (по выбору — strict gating)
- **Counterfeit removal** via Brand Registry violation reporting
- **Trademark enforcement** integration

В Vladimir's case: все 5 Amazon accounts (Salutem, Personal, AMZ Commerce, Sirius, Retailer) — listed как **authorized sellers** в Brand Registry. Это блокирует посторонних sellers на Salutem Vita ASINs.

### 2. A+ Content access

- Enhanced product descriptions с rich media
- Modules: Banner, Comparison Chart, Lifestyle Imagery, Q&A
- **Conversion uplift +5-10%** (Amazon's reported data)

Bundle Factory Phase 2 — A+ Content generation для top-performing bundles.

### 3. Brand Story

- Customizable brand banner на каждой product page
- Cross-sell other products в каталоге
- Links к Brand Store

### 4. Sponsored Brand Ads

- PPC ad type специально для brand-owners
- Multi-product ad placements
- Brand-tracked attribution

### 5. Brand Store

- Free customizable storefront amazon.com/{brand}
- Hero banner, product categories, lifestyle imagery
- URL: amazon.com/salutemvita (если зарезервирован)

### 6. GTIN Exemption

См. [`gtin-exemption-process.md`](gtin-exemption-process.md). Только Brand Registry owners могут apply.

### 7. Virtual Bundles

Brand Registry owners могут создавать **Virtual Bundles** — combine 2-5 existing ASINs в single display listing без physical packaging / new UPC. Phase 2 feature для Vladimir.

## Bundle Factory implications

1. **Salutem Vita bundles** должны быть created на Salutem Solutions account первой (brand owner). Затем cross-listed через authorized seller accounts.
2. **Starfit bundles** — создаются на Sirius International.
3. **GTIN exemption** — apply на каждый brand × category combination.
4. **A+ Content** — Phase 2 priority (Vladimir сначала focus на quantity scaling).
