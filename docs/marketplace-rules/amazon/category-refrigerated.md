## TL;DR

Refrigerated grocery (cheese, deli meats, fresh dips, eggs) — близко к Frozen но менее strict. Также требует Ungating. Cooler + gel packs необходимы, но short transit time допустим.

---

## Hard rules

### 1. Storage temperature

`storage_temperature: Refrigerated` обязательно для:
- Cheese & charcuterie
- Deli meats (Boar's Head, Hillshire)
- Fresh dips (hummus, ranch)
- Yogurt / kefir
- Refrigerated dough (Pillsbury)

### 2. Approval требуется

Аналогично Frozen — ungating per account. Verify status.

### 3. Shipping requirements

- Cooler box (smaller OK для refrigerated vs frozen)
- 1-2 gel packs
- Same-day or Next-day shipping
- Mon-Wed only

### 4. Allergens, expiration — те же что Frozen

См. [`category-frozen-grocery.md`](category-frozen-grocery.md).
