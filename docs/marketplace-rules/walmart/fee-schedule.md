# Walmart Marketplace — Fee Schedule

> **Source:** https://sellercentral.walmart.com/help
> **Last verified:** 2026-05-17
> **Priority:** P1

---

## TL;DR

Walmart Marketplace берёт **referral fee 12-15% для grocery** (similar to Amazon's 15% above $15). Не имеет variable closing fee. Lower fees overall чем Amazon, но меньше traffic.

---

## Fee structure

### Referral Fee per category

| Category | Walmart Referral Fee |
|---|---|
| **Grocery (Food)** | 8% (≤$10), 12% (>$10) |
| Pet Food & Supplies | 15% |
| Health & Beauty | 15% |
| Baby (food) | 12% |
| General Merchandise | 15% |

Comparison vs Amazon:
- Grocery $15 sale: Amazon 8% = $1.20; Walmart 12% = $1.80
- Grocery $60 sale: Amazon 15% = $9.00; Walmart 12% = $7.20

Walmart cheaper для $40+ sales. Amazon cheaper для small items.

### No variable closing fee

Unlike Amazon Media categories — Walmart не имеет дополнительных fees.

### Refund fee

20% от refund amount, minimum $5.00 — same as Amazon.

## Vladimir's profitability adaptation

Sample shelf-stable bundle $60:

| Item | Amazon | Walmart |
|---|---|---|
| Sale | $60.00 | $60.00 |
| Referral fee | -$9.00 (15%) | -$7.20 (12%) |
| Components | -$18.00 | -$18.00 |
| Packaging | -$2.00 | -$2.00 |
| Shipping | -$8.00 | -$8.00 |
| **Net** | **$23.00** | **$24.80** |

Walmart **margin slightly better** на shelf-stable bundles. Это аргумент для активного development Walmart channel.
