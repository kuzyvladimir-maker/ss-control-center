## Application steps

### Step 1: Account creation

- Visit seller-us.tiktok.com
- Create seller account (separate from personal TikTok creator account)
- Verify email + phone

### Step 2: Business verification

Required documents:
- **Business registration:** LLC paperwork (Salutem Solutions LLC, Sirius International LLC)
- **EIN (Tax ID):** для each business entity
- **Address verification:** lease agreement или utility bill at warehouse
- **Bank account:** для payouts
- **W-9 form:** US tax compliance

Vladimir's status:
- Account 1 (TIKTOK_1) — TBD (нужно apply на Salutem Solutions LLC)
- Account 2 (TIKTOK_2) — TBD (на Sirius International LLC или other entity)

### Step 3: Category selection

Apply для specific categories:
- **Food & Beverage** — primary
- **Gifting** — sub-category for gift sets

Each category может require separate approval.

### Step 4: Product compliance review

Initial product listings submitted, TikTok team reviews:
- Brand authenticity (Salutem Vita brand docs)
- Image quality
- Title compliance
- Pricing reasonableness
- Inventory availability

Review timeline: **2-4 weeks per submission round**.

### Step 5: Performance probation

После approval — 90-day probation period:
- Performance metrics tracked: fulfillment rate, customer rating, refund rate
- Если metrics fall — restrictions added
- Если metrics good — restrictions lifted

### Step 6: Affiliate program access

После 30-60 days of good performance:
- Unlock TikTok Affiliate program
- Open products для creator promotion (Vladimir sets commission %)
- This где TikTok shines — viral product placement через creators

---

## Common rejection reasons

1. **Insufficient brand documentation** — fix: provide Brand Registry certificates
2. **Foreign brand IP в bundle title** — fix: rewrite titles per [`../amazon/gift-set-policy.md`](../amazon/gift-set-policy.md)
3. **Quality concerns** — fix: better images, more product info
4. **Pricing too aggressive** — fix: set pricing similar to other channels
5. **Account history issues** — fix: clean account, no previous violations
